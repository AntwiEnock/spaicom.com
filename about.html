WHO WE ARE

Spaicom Company Limited is a Ghanaian based engineering company that specializes in the provision of Telecom and rehabilitation solutions for organizations across the globe. Our services range from land acquisition to procurement of telecom materials, site surveys, construction, site commissioning, and site maintenance. Our major objective is to execute turnkey projects in the Telecom mobile infrastructure industry, and here at Spaicom Company Limited, we are confident our wide array of useful and cost-effective services and our dedication to exceeding your expectations on a daily basis will enable us to provide you that quality and expert service you desire. We have been in operation for over seven years having received our certificate of incorporation from the republic of Ghana on 7th January 2016. With over 35 full time and 200-part time staff strategically spread across the country, we have the capacity of handling countless number of BTS sites per month. This is why we believe doing business with us is the best decision you can ever take for your organization.
