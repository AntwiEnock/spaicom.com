CUSTOMER FOCUS

 Develop and maintain an Estimating Management System.
 Be selective in choosing our customers and type of projects.
 Ensure that SPAICOM has the ability to meet our customers’ requirements.
 Select suppliers and subcontractors based on their ability to meet our customer’s requirement
 Review project tenders to ensure that our customers' requirements are adequately defined and clearly documented.
 Ensure our customers’ requirements are communicated to all relevant employees before initiating the project.
 Deliver projects according to our customers' contract specifications.
 Provide innovative solutions on complex and demanding projects.
 Maintain a mutually beneficial relationship with selected customers.
 Understand our customers' current and future needs.
 Undertake a customer satisfaction survey at the completion of each project.

BUSINESS STRATEGY

Our organization has been segmented into various engineering departments, with each department having its own strategic plans moulded around the market in which it operates. Our company employs only the best from the top schools in the country and beyond with exceptional expertise. This has helped us to increase our exposure in the Ghanaian market and the African continent at large by adding at least one major operator to our client list every year. Over the years we have strengthened our relationship with both local and foreign clients; because our staff consists of a beautiful blend of the several ethnicities in Ghana. This has made it very easy for us to work in every local area without any hindrances and barriers. We also have several Telecom materials and equipment from world accredited manufacturers in our local warehouses. Our materials and equipment come with an additional certification from our suppliers. Some of the materials and equipment in our warehouses include Generators, ATS, AVR, Rectifier and backup systems, shelters, Indoor electrical reticulations, Air conditioners, Fencing materials, Canopies, Fuel tanks, Towers, Guyed masts, Monopoles, RF materials, Antennas, Feeder cables, Connectors
