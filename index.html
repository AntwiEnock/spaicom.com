Spaicom

Spaicom Company Limited is a Ghanaian based engineering company that specializes in the provision of Telecom and rehabilitation solutions for organizations across the globe. Our services range from land acquisition to procurement of telecom materials, site surveys, construction, site commissioning, and site maintenance. Our major objective is to execute turnkey projects in the Telecom mobile infrastructure industry, and here at Spaicom Company Limited, we are confident our wide array of useful and cost-effective services and our dedication to exceeding your expectations on a daily basis will enable us to provide you that quality and expert service you desire.
